## Naming Convention Rules
- No version numbers in filenames (use frontmatter)
- Lowercase with hyphens
- Descriptive but concise
- No abbreviations unless standard (API, SDK, CLI)
- No emojis or special characters

## Document Structure Template
```markdown
# [Document Title]

## Executive Summary
[2-3 paragraph overview]

## Table of Contents
[Auto-generated]

## Introduction
[Context and scope]

## Core Concepts
[Key definitions and principles]

## Technical Specification
[Detailed technical content]

## Implementation
[Practical guidance]

## References
[Academic citations]

## Appendices
[Supplementary material]
```

## Rewrite Guidelines
1. Academic tone without being inaccessible
2. Precision in technical terminology
3. Clear hierarchical structure
4. Consistent formatting
5. No casual language
6. No marketing speak
7. Evidence-based claims
8. Proper citations
